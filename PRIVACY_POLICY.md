# Privacy Policy

**Text to Calendar** Chrome Extension

*Last Updated: January 2025*

---

## Overview

Text to Calendar is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data stays on your device.

---

## Data Collection

### What We Store

The extension stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Event history (titles, dates, times)
- Parsed event details from text you've highlighted
- Your usage preferences

### What We Don't Collect

- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No analytics or usage metrics
- No identifying information of any kind

---

## Data Transmission

### External Connections

The extension makes **no connections to external servers** except:

- **Google Calendar** (calendar.google.com) â€” Only when you choose to create an event. This opens Google's standard calendar interface in a new tab. This action is always initiated by you.

### No Data Sent

- No data is transmitted to us or any third party
- No background network requests
- No telemetry or crash reports

---

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage API:

- Data **never leaves your browser**
- Data is tied to your Chrome profile
- Data persists until you clear it

### Clearing Your Data

You can delete all stored data at any time:

1. Click the extension icon
2. Click "Clear History"
3. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, removing the extension will delete all associated data.

---

## Third Parties

- **No third-party analytics** (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels)
- **No advertising** of any kind
- **No data sharing** with any third parties
- **No external dependencies** that collect data

---

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests these permissions:

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `contextMenus` | To add "Create Calendar Event" to the right-click menu |
| `storage` | To save your event history locally |
| `notifications` | To show a brief confirmation when an event is created |

No permission is used to collect or transmit your data.
